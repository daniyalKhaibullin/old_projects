Game Rules

Welcome to our ping pong game! The objective of the game is to score points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle.

Here are the rules:

  1. The game is played on a table divided by a net.
  2. Each player controls a paddle on their side of the table.
  3. The player on the left controls their paddle using the W and S keys to move up and down, respectively.
  4. The player on the right controls their paddle using the I and K keys to move up and down, respectively.
  5. A point is scored when one player fails to return the ball within the bounds of the opponent's side of the table.
  6. The first player to reach a predetermined number of points 11 wins the game.
